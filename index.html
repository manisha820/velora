The Heritage

Velora  —  Born  from  the  philosophy
that  luxury  is  not  worn,
it  is  inhabited.

Founded in the golden quarter of Paris by master perfumer Elara Voss, VELORA was conceived as a rejection of the ordinary. Where others produced fragrance, she architected atmosphere — invisible rooms you carry wherever you go.

Today, each VELORA creation takes between three and seven years to perfect. There is no compromise. There is no shortcut. There is only the pursuit of the singular, the unforgettable, the  royal.

— Elara Voss, Fondatrice
